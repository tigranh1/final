About us

Tumo Labs Student Union - created by the students and for the students.

A Student Union is a student-led organization that represents the students at TUMO Labs.

We understood that the staff had little time, so we have decided to coordinate in order to organize leisure for ourselves, help develop the community and cooperate with other organizations at the system level

Our mission

Our mission is to promote and protect students' rights by forming a community of students who want to hear and be heard by others. Union members help others achieve their goals and simultaneously set long-term goals for the community as a whole.



Our Union values are:

T

Tact

Each Union member must be polite, respect the opinions of other members and all students

U

Unity

Each Union member should help the others in difficult situations and in the same way, the rest will help him/her

M

Motivation

Union members must be motivated to improve the workflow of Tumo Labs, suggest ideas, or organize events for students to make education better.

O

Opportunity

Union is an opportunity to find like-minded people who like what you like too. Working together is always easier and more productive



Benefits

What do you get as a Union member?

  1. You can promote exactly what you are interested in - sports, games, art, music events, workshops organizations & more
  2. Collaboration with other companies - Union will work with local and national companies to give you discounts on services products and services they offer
  3. Opportunity to become a leader

Everyone can become a member of the Student Union, regardless of where he studies or works.

All you need to do is to fill out the form (by submitting you will only declare your desire to know more about how to become a member). After that, you will be contacted at the phone number or email you specified for further actions
